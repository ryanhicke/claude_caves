## Pro Tips & Secrets

### Combat Tips
- **Only one weapon exists**: The shiny dagger in The Arsenal
- **Weapon is required**: You cannot defeat the guardian without it
- **No health system**: Combat is instant win/lose based on equipment

### Navigation Tips
- Use the `map` command frequently
- The `look` command refreshes room descriptions
- Direction shortcuts (n/s/e/w) speed up movement
- You can type just the direction (no need for "go")

### Exploration Tips
- Visit all rooms before the final confrontation
- Read all descriptions - they add atmosphere and lore
- The Warning Chamber sign is the only critical clue
- Some rooms connect in unexpected ways

### Speedrun Strategy

1. `north` - Go to Arsenal

3. `east` - Go to Warning Chamber
